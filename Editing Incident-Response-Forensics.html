Incident Response & Forensics

Rapid Containment, Evidence Preservation, and Secure Recovery

Immediate Containment

We act immediately to stop ongoing attacker activity and reduce operational impact. The first objective is to prevent lateral movement, data exfiltration, and re-infection.

  • Isolation: segment affected hosts, disable compromised accounts, restrict remote access.
  • Blocking: firewall/EDR containment actions, IOC blocking, suspicious domain/IP filtering.
  • Evidence-first: preserve volatile data (memory/process/network) before any destructive action.
  • Coordination: align IT, security, and business stakeholders with a clear action plan.
Contain fast, preserve evidence, restore control.

Digital Forensics

Our forensic investigation determines what happened, how it happened, and what was impacted. We reconstruct the attacker timeline and validate scope with defensible methods (integrity, traceability, and chain-of-custody where required).

  • Collection: endpoints/servers images, cloud/identity logs, EDR telemetry, network traces.
  • Analysis: persistence, privilege escalation, lateral movement, exfiltration indicators.
  • Attribution support: IOCs, TTP mapping, campaign clustering (when feasible).
  • Outcome: incident timeline, root cause, impacted assets, and evidence package.
Correlate evidence into a defensible incident narrative.

GDPR-Compliant Notification

We help you assess whether the incident qualifies as a personal data breach and prepare compliant communication packages. This includes impact assessment, scope validation, and documentation needed for regulators and stakeholders.

  • Assessment: personal data exposure, affected data subjects, risk severity.
  • Documentation: incident summary, timeline, controls in place, remediation actions.
  • Notification support: regulator-facing wording and evidence alignment.
  • Consistency: ensure communications match forensic findings and recovery actions.
Align legal obligations with verified facts and evidence.
