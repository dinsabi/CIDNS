Penetration Testing & Red Teaming

Real-World Offensive Security to Validate Exposure and Improve Resilience

Network and Application Tests

We assess your exposure through structured penetration tests across networks, infrastructure, and applications. The goal is to identify exploitable vulnerabilities, validate impact, and provide actionable remediation guidance.

  • Network: external/internal testing, segmentation validation, privilege escalation paths.
  • Applications: web/mobile/API testing, authentication/authorization flaws, OWASP-aligned checks.
  • Cloud/Identity (optional): misconfigurations, IAM weaknesses, tenant hardening opportunities.
  • Evidence-based: exploit validation where safe, with clear impact and reproducible steps.
Infrastructure + application coverage ‚Üí validated findings.

Realistic Simulations

Red Team exercises simulate realistic attacker behavior to test not only vulnerabilities, but also your ability to detect, respond, and contain. We emulate real-world tactics to measure resilience across people, process, and technology.

  • Objective-based: access a specific system, reach sensitive data, or validate security controls.
  • Stealth & realism: TTP-driven scenarios (phishing, lateral movement, persistence) when in scope.
  • Blue Team validation: measure detection, triage speed, response effectiveness, and escalation paths.
  • Safe execution: strict rules of engagement, scope boundaries, and business-friendly scheduling.
Measure both exposure and detection/response readiness.

Mitigation Recommendations

We translate findings into a prioritized remediation plan that reduces real risk and strengthens your security baseline. Recommendations are pragmatic, mapped to business impact, and aligned with best practices.

  • Prioritization: critical paths first (exposure + exploitability + business impact).
  • Fix guidance: configuration changes, patching, secure coding, segmentation, identity hardening.
  • Control uplift: logging/monitoring improvements, EDR tuning, MFA/conditional access, secure backups.
  • Deliverables: executive summary + technical report + replay steps + remediation roadmap.
Clear actions mapped to impact and effort.
