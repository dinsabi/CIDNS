Ensuring Strategic Cloud Adoption and Operational Excellence

üîç Service Overview

CIDNS acts as a strategic partner to guide cloud journeys with clarity and control. By selecting the right provider and embedding governance from the start, organizations gain faster delivery, lower risk, and sustained compliance.

This service is typically structured around two key pillars:

1. Assist in Cloud Provider Selection

This phase involves helping organizations evaluate and select the most suitable cloud provider(s) based on their business, technical, security, and financial requirements.

  • Assessment of business and technical needs (workload profiles, resilience, availability, scalability, budget)
  • Benchmarking AWS, Azure, GCP, OVH, etc. by:
    • Technical capabilities: PaaS, Kubernetes, AI/ML, storage, databases
    • Compliance readiness: ISO 27001, GDPR, HDS
    • Pricing models and cost optimizations
    • Data residency and geographic footprint
  • Vendor lock-in risk assessment and mitigation
  • Recommendations: single, hybrid or multi-cloud strategies

2. Implement IT Governance: Security, Compliance, Supervision

This component ensures robust governance is in place to meet compliance, security and monitoring requirements.

  • Cloud Security Governance:
    • IAM and role-based access control
    • Data encryption in transit and at rest
    • KMS and key lifecycle management (BYOK, HSMs)
    • Network security via VPNs, bastions, segmentation
  • Compliance Alignment:
    • Alignment to GDPR, PCI-DSS, ISO 27001, etc.
    • Audit logging (CloudTrail, Azure Monitor)
    • Compliance automation (Azure Policy, AWS Config)
  • Supervision & Control:
    • Full observability: logs, metrics, tracing
    • Cloud-native & third-party monitoring (Datadog, Grafana)
    • CMDB and asset discovery
    • FinOps: cloud usage and cost governance
