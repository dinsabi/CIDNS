Service Definition

At CIDNS, every mission begins with a comprehensive assessment of your IT infrastructure and business needs. This fundamental step helps us understand your technical landscape, constraints, and goals to build a secure and compliant strategy.

Objectives

  • Identify technical weaknesses and vulnerabilities
  • Adapt your IT strategy to comply with NIS2, GDPR, ISO 27001 regulations
  • Facilitate a secure cloud migration or reinforce cybersecurity posture

Client Benefits

  • Improved visibility over your IT assets and critical applications
  • Thorough risk evaluation from both technical and organizational perspectives
  • Better alignment with compliance and regulatory standards
  • Informed decision-making for your digital or cloud transformation roadmap

Deliverables

  • Comprehensive audit report with executive summary
  • Simplified IT and application mapping
  • Strategic recommendations focusing on performance, security, and compliance
